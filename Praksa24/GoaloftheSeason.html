Goal of the season in the Premier League 23/24

The Argentinian's strike received the most combined votes by the public and a panel of football experts, topping a shortlist that featured each of the season's Budweiser Goal of the Month winners, plus one standout strike from May. His goal beat those of Kaoru Mitoma, Bruno Fernandes, Saman Ghoddos, Alexis Mac Allister, Oscar Bobb, Kobbie Mainoo, Marcus Rashford, Cole Palmer and Moises Caicedo. It is the first time a Man Utd player has won the award since its inception in 2016/17.
